ITD Day Celebration 2026

The ITD Day Celebration is a special event for the Information Technology Department of Cavite State University - Naic Campus. This year's theme is Super Mario, making the celebration fun, colorful, and exciting.

The event includes various games, performances, and competitions that showcase the talents of students. It also gives recognition to those who worked hard during the school year.

Activities

  • Mobile Legends Competition
  • Tech Expo 2026
  • Energizer Games
  • ITD Got Talent 2026
  • Awarding Ceremony
